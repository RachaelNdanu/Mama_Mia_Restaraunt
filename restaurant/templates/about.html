About Us

Located in the heart of Westlands, Nairobi, Mama Mia brings the soul of the Mediterranean to Kenya. We specialize in authentic Italian, Greek, and Turkish cuisine prepared with a fresh modern flair.

Our rotating seasonal menu features 12–15 curated dishes made from quality local and imported ingredients, ensuring every visit is a new experience.

Whether you're joining us for a light lunch, a cozy dinner, or a celebration, Mama Mia offers a warm, rustic atmosphere with exceptional hospitality.

Passionate about taste and tradition, our kitchen is led by two of Nairobi’s most talented head chefs who infuse every plate with excellence and heart.
